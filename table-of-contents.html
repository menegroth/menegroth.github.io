Table of Contents

This text is divided into four sections: two short and introductory, two long and in-depth. The first two, The Tengwar Writing System and Speaking Sindarin, will give you a taste of the language, and a basic understanding of many of the elvish names, phrases, and inscriptions in The Lord of the Rings. The third section, the Grammar of Sindarin, is a deeper look at the rules that govern the construction of words and phrases. Finally, there is the History of Sindarin, which describes how the languages of the Elves changed through the long histories of Middle-earth and of Aman in the West.
