Translation Requests

If you want to know what some piece of Elvish text means, email contact.menegroth@gmail.com and I'll try to help you out. Unfortunately, I can't do translations from English to Elvish. The basic reason for this is that I can't speak Elvish, because Tolkien's languages are intentionally not designed to be spoken fluently. Any translations therefore require an adjustment (some would say a distortion) of the known facts about the language, and I can't scrupulously give you an adjusted version of the language without a mountain of commentary.
